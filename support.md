# Discover — Support

Need help with Discover? We're here for you.

## Contact Us

For questions, feedback, or support requests, email us at:

**[discover.app.support@gmail.com](mailto:discover.app.support@gmail.com)**

We aim to respond within 48 hours.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to Settings → Manage Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all saved data. You can also visit our [account deletion page](./delete-account).

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings → Manage Subscription. This will open your device's subscription management where you can cancel anytime.

**I found a bug — how do I report it?**
Use the "Report a Problem" option in Settings, or email us directly at the address above.

**Can I use Discover without an account?**
Yes! You can browse and swipe through places without signing in. An account is only needed to save places to your collections.
