# Delete Your Account

**Discover**

## How to Delete Your Account in the App

1. Open the Discover app
2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Tap **Manage Account**
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm your identity (enter your password, or re-authenticate with Google)
6. Confirm the deletion

Your account will be permanently deleted immediately.

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is **permanently removed**:

- Your Firebase authentication account (email, sign-in credentials)
- All saved places and collections stored in our database
- Your subscription status and purchase history link (via RevenueCat)
- Locally stored data including swipe history, search history, and swipe counts

## What Is Not Stored (and Therefore Not Deleted)

- **Location data** — Discover never stores your location on our servers. It is only used on-device to calculate distances.
- **Swipe history** — Stored only on your device and expires automatically after 3 days.

## Can't Access the App?

If you have uninstalled Discover or cannot sign in, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

**discover.app.support@gmail.com**

Please include the email address you used to create your account. We will verify your identity and delete your account and all associated data within 7 business days.
